Nordstrom.com Usability Test Report

Tasks

1. Find a pair of shoes on sale for a first date.

User began by searching for formal shoes and immediately reacted to several options as “too fancy” and somewhat expensive. They applied filters to narrow results to sale items and continued browsing. After comparing a few options, they selected a more reasonable pair and added a size 6M to the cart. The user seemed slightly unsure at first but became more confident once filters were applied.

Time: 40.96 secs

2. Find a gift under $100.

User searched for plush gift items and quickly became excited while browsing, reacting positively to multiple options. They specifically showed interest in a dragon plush, commenting on how “cute” it was and checking the price to ensure it was under $100. The user had no difficulty navigating and completed the task quickly, even continuing to browse plush items out of interest.

Time: 24.57 secs

3. Find the highest-priced wall art.

User searched for plush gift items and quickly became excited while browsing, reacting positively to multiple options. They specifically showed interest in a dragon plush, commenting on how “cute” it was and checking the price to ensure it was under $100. The user had no difficulty navigating and completed the task quickly, even continuing to browse plush items out of interest.

Time: 52.23 secs

4. Find wedding-appropriate apparel.

User navigated to the wall art section and began scrolling through available products. They expressed surprise at some of the prices and spent time looking around but did not use the sorting feature to organize items by price. As a result, they did not identify the highest-priced item and relied only on manual scrolling, which made the task less efficient.

Time: 45.12 secs

5. Book a styling appointment.

User attempted to book an in-store styling appointment and first verified the store location. They scrolled to the footer expecting to find the service but were unable to locate it there. The user then explored multiple navigation headers before eventually finding the styling appointment option. They appeared slightly nervous during this process but successfully completed the booking after some trial and error.

Time: 140.52 secs
